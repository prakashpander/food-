About Us

We've been Making Delicious Food for Last 10 Years

Rajasthani food is a flavorful reflection of the state's desert culture, featuring bold spices and hearty dishes. Signature recipes like Dal Baati Churma, Gatte Ki Sabzi, and Ker Sangri showcase its rich heritage. Paired with Bajre Ki Roti, these meals are comforting, rustic, and often accompanied by delightful sweets like Ghevar.
